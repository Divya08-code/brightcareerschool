our history


In 2002, the Bright Career Academy was established by Pujya Pad Swami Shivom Teerth Shiksha Prasar and Samaj Kalyan Samiti in Ambah tehsil of Morena district of Madhya Pradesh. Dr. Bhagwan Sahai Sharma and his mother Smt Anar Devi Upadhyay, professor and language penetrator of Hindi subject in Ambah Postgraduate College, laid the foundation.Dr.Pragati Sharma on the post of Principal and Dr. Sudhir Acharya and Sushil Kumar Physiotherapist as Director have been continuously performing their duties.Presently the chairperson of the committee is Mrs. Pushpa Devi Sharma Aruddha.In view of the increase in the capacity of the students in 2004, Bhoomi Poojan for the huge private building was done by the then Commissioner of State Education Center, Bhopal, Shri Avdhesh Narayan Tiwari on 2 February 2004, near Pachasa Maidan, Police Quarters.At present the Academy has 35 teaching rooms, a library, four laboratories (chemistry, physics, biology and mathematics), two computer rooms, music hall, smart class, dance class, taekwondo class including two large activity halls and sports complex.







WHY BRIGHT CAREER ACADEMY?


Bright Career School came into existence in the year 2002 and has made wonderful progress. It has become a full fledged tree blossoming in full swing, spreading its fragrance. The English medium co- educational,Senior Secondary School run by Dr.Pragati Sharma and Dr.Sudhir Acharya.
