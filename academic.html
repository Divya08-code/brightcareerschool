EXAM PATTERN


  • Examination pattern: The Academy consists of quarterly examination , half yearly examination , pre annual exam and annual exam along with monthly test.
  • The academy offers its students the opportunity to prepare test papers themselves and incorporate them into the valuation pattern. After the examinations, the candidates get an opportunity to check their copies so that they can get to know about their mistakes and at the same time the parents can also check the copies of the examinations of their children, with the help of this policy there will be no chances of partiality.
